About me and the Collective

My path of self-growth started when, after hitting rock bottom, I came to the realisation that absolutely all good and bad in my life ties to the choices I make.

This level of responsibility means that if I can create it all, I can change it too - I took my power back!

This led to years of self-development, hundreds of books and all sorts of interesting exploration.

I turned my life around and in the process I empowered many women to do the same.

The Empower me Collective is about building a collective of like-minded people who wants to grow, learn form each other and courageously embark on a path to achieve what we want in life.

It is a privilege to share what I learnt with you and I thank you for joining me on this journey. :)
